Nanotechnology in drug delivery has been proven by nanoparticles that can have very unique properties both in vitro and in vivo, especially when targeting drug delivery to tumors. Heart Disease Treatment

Nanotechnology is also being used to advance and improve heart disease treatments. Coronary artery disease is the progressive plaque formation on the major arteries that can lead to heart failure. Nanomedicine is a valid treatment in this situation since the development of this specific disease occurs at the cellular level. The current methods out there today of coronary artery disease consists of using statins, known to cause side effects which limit their application. Nanotechnology can provide a more effective and effecient solution.

Solar Panels

Nanotechnology can dramatically improve design and manufacturing of PV films. However, nanotechnology within this industry will really shine when it comes to solar cell technologies, where novel technologies such as nanowires, quantum dots, as well as radial junctions will come together to really improve the PV efficiency. Nanostructures can also allow efficient solar cellsto be made from cheaper, more traditional materials, like silicon and titanium dioxide. Although there will be cost barriers involved in developing mass production techniques for nano-enhanced PV cells, the use of cheaper raw materials will allow the cost of commercial solar cells to continue to decrease.
